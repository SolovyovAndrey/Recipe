Mike's Hot Honey BBQ Ribs

Ingredients

  • 2 racks baby back ribs
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup Mike's Hot Honey
  • Optional: chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F (150°C).
  2. Remove the thin membrane from the back of the ribs for tenderness.
  3. Season both sides of the ribs with salt and pepper.
  4. Wrap ribs in foil and place on a baking sheet. Bake for 2.5–3 hours until tender.
  5. Mix BBQ sauce and Mike's Hot Honey in a bowl.
  6. Remove ribs from oven, discard foil, and brush generously with the honey BBQ sauce.
  7. Grill or broil ribs for 5–10 minutes until sauce is caramelized and sticky.
  8. Slice, garnish with parsley if desired, and serve.
