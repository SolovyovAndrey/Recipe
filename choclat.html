Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt & freshly ground black pepper
  • Fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain.
  2. While the pasta cooks, fry the pancetta in a large pan over medium heat until crispy. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add the drained spaghetti to the pan with pancetta. Remove from heat.
  5. Quickly pour in the egg and cheese mixture, tossing rapidly to coat the pasta (the heat will cook the eggs and create a creamy sauce).
  6. If needed, add a splash of reserved pasta water to loosen the sauce.
  7. Season with salt and plenty of black pepper. Serve immediately, garnished with parsley and extra Parmesan.
